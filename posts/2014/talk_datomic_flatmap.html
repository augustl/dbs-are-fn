date: 2014.06.28 title: Talk: The imminent revolution of functional append-only databases

I presented at flatMap 2014 in Oslo. My main point was that old data is valuable to many domains, and that keeping old data shouldn't be your problem. It is quite difficult to get versioning of data right in key/value stores, document databases and RDBMSes. Databases like Event Store and Datomic solves this problem for you.
