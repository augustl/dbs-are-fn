title: About this blog date: 2013.09.10

DB's are fn. Pun intended!

fn denotes a function in Clojure.

(fn [a b] (+ a  b))

Most Clojure programmers refers to fn's using the english word fun.

Fun is the enjoyment of pleasure, particularly in leisure activities. Fun is an experience - short-term, often unexpected, informal, not cerebral and generally purposeless. It is an enjoyable distraction, diverting the mind and body from any serious task or contributing an extra dimension to it. Wikipedia

This blog is more about simplicity than fun, though. In fact, you'll might get some blisters from reading this blog. But blisters never hurt anyone.

Datomic is the only contestant I'm aware of in the functional database space, and it's the database I'll be writing about. If a challenger apperas, I will certainly write about it, too.

Have fn!
